Manifestation 101: Living in the End

"Living in the end" means embodying the feeling of your wish fulfilled. It's not about "faking it," but about genuinely shifting your internal state to match the reality you want to create. When you think, feel, and act from that desired state, you align your energy with it, making you a magnet for the experiences and opportunities that will bring it to life.
